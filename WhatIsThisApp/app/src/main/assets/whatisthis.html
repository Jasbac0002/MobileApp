What is this App

This app is a quiz app that tests your knowledge about fruits and animals found in New Zealand.

Explore the rich diversity of New Zealand's flora and fauna through a fun and educational quiz experience.

Quiz Categories

  • Fruits
  • Animals

Features

  • Multiple-choice questions
  • Score tracking
  • Interactive and engaging user interface

How to Play

  1. Choose a category (fruits or animals)
  2. Answer the multiple-choice questions
  3. Submit your answers and see your score
  4. Try to improve your score by playing again

Learn and Have Fun!

This app is not only educational but also entertaining.

Discover interesting facts about various fruits and animals that call New Zealand home.

Expand your knowledge while enjoying an immersive quiz experience.
